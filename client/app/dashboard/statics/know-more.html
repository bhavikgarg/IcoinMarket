 Bitcoin

Bitcoin is the first digital currency of the world. It is the most popular, notable and highest in market capitalization.

Initial price value of bitcoin in July 2010 was $ 0.008. In first five days, the price grew 1000%, rising from $ 0.008 to $ 0.08 per bitcoin. After 1 year in July 2011 its price reached $31.00. Current bitcoin price is around $800-$ 1,150.



 Ethereum

After bitcoin, the most popular coin is Ethereum using smart contract feature. Its price reached all time high on 16 June 2016, with US$1.6B of market capitalization.

Ethereum initial price value on 07 Aug 2015 was $ 2.83. After one year on 14 March 2016, the price reached $13.82. The price value soar to $17.69 in 17 June 2016, Latest price on 22 Feb 2017 was $0.196772.
